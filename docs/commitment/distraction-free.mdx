## Experience accelerated learning progress with eltepi's innovative use of the memory palace technique, proven to improve retention of new vocabulary

### No Adds

At eltepi, we understand that interruptions and distractions can be major hindrances to learning, which is why we've made the decision not to include random third-party ads in our app.

### No inappropriate or irrelevant content

This means you won't have to worry about being interrupted by ads for violent zombie shooters or other inappropriate or irrelevant content.

### Focused and Clean

Our goal is to create a clean and focused learning environment for students, and we believe that any type of distraction would be contradictory to that goal. By keeping our app and content free from interruptions, we hope to help students develop the skills they need to focus and retain new information.

If you're tired of learning tools that are cluttered with distractions and ads, give eltepi a try and see how our commitment to a clean and focused learning experience can make a difference in your language learning journey.
